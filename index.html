Setting Engineering Standards

Fueling Mechanical Innovation at IIT Kharagpur

Mechanika, IIT Kharagpur's mechanical engineering society, fuels innovation and excellence. Our mission nurtures a vibrant community of enthusiasts, fostering learning, pioneering projects, and impactful initiatives. Rooted in academic brilliance, collaboration, and leadership, we empower members through workshops, tech competitions, and industry partnerships. From inventive projects to industry acknowledgment, our achievements underscore our dedication to shaping the future of mechanical engineering.
